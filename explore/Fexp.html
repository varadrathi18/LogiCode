🎉 Festivals & Traditions

Bastille Day

Celebrated on July 14th, France’s national day marks the start of the French Revolution with fireworks and parades.

Fête de la Musique

A nationwide celebration of music , where streets come alive with free concerts and performances.

🍽 Traditional Food

Croissants & Pastries

Flaky, buttery croissants are a staple of French breakfasts, often enjoyed with coffee or jam.

Coq au Vin

A traditional dish of chicken slow-cooked in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and bacon — full of rich flavor.

🏛 Famous Landmarks

Eiffel Tower

The most iconic symbol of France, standing tall in the heart of Paris and offering breathtaking city views.

Mont Saint-Michel

A magical island commune in Normandy with a medieval abbey that rises dramatically above the sea.

💡 Fun Facts

  • 🇫🇷 French is spoken on five continents and is one of the official languages of the UN.
  • 🧀 France produces over 1,200 types of cheese — that’s a lot of fromage!
  • 🥖 The French baguette is so iconic it’s even recognized by UNESCO as cultural heritage.
