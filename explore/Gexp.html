🎉 Festivals & Traditions

Oktoberfest

Experience the world’s largest beer festival held in Munich with traditional costumes, music, and parades.

Christmas Markets

German Weihnachtsmärkte bring magical lights, crafts, mulled wine, and festive treats in towns across the country.

🍽 Traditional Food

Bratwurst & Sauerkraut

Classic grilled sausages served with fermented cabbage — a beloved German staple.

Pretzels & Beer

Enjoy soft pretzels with mustard or butter, often paired with a cold mug of German beer.

🏛 Famous Landmarks

Brandenburg Gate

Berlin’s iconic 18th-century neoclassical monument, symbolizing unity and peace.

Neuschwanstein Castle

The fairytale castle in Bavaria that inspired Disney's Sleeping Beauty castle.

💡 Fun Facts

  • 🇩🇪 German is the most widely spoken native language in Europe.
  • 🌍 The word "Fernweh" means a deep craving for travel — like the opposite of homesickness.
  • 🍞 Germany has over 300 varieties of bread, and even a bread museum!
