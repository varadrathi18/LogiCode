🎉 Festivals & Traditions

La Tomatina

A famous tomato-throwing festival held every August in Buñol, drawing visitors from all over the world.

Feria de Abril

Seville's April Fair is full of flamenco dancing, traditional dress, horse parades, and vibrant colors.

🍽 Traditional Food

Paella

This saffron-flavored rice dish is often made with seafood, chicken, or rabbit and is a Spanish culinary icon.

Tapas

Small dishes that are perfect for sharing — from olives to spicy potatoes and chorizo to grilled prawns.

🏛 Famous Landmarks

Sagrada Família

An iconic basilica in Barcelona designed by Gaudí, still under construction after over a century.

Alhambra

A majestic palace and fortress complex in Granada known for its Islamic architecture and lush gardens.

💡 Fun Facts

  • 🇪🇸 Spanish is the second-most spoken language in the world by native speakers.
  • 🎭 Flamenco is not just a dance, but an art form that includes singing and guitar playing.
  • 🌍 Spain has 48 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, one of the highest numbers globally.
